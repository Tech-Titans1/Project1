About Us

Veggie Delight was imagined and built up from a place of passion and from a concept of consciousness for sustainable living.

Know where your food is coming from

Our vegetable garden has been cultivated organically, grown around our core ethos of producing and supplying sustainable and healthy food that promote better health and well-being. Our main principles are health, fairness and care. These principles apply to the environment in which the food is grown and those that consume the food.


Our goal is to protect the health of the living organisms, that includes people and those who live in the environment.
